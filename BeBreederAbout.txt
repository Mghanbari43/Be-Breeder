############################
#### About Be-Breeder  #####
############################

Be-Breeder is an app developed by the Laboratory of Allogamous Plant Breeding, 
Department of Genetics, Luiz de Queiroz College of Agriculture, University of
Sao Paulo, Brazil. 

Be-Breeder is a click point graphical interface developed by the 
P.hD. student Filipe Inacio Matias and Professor Roberto Fritsche Neto
using Shiny package in R software for statistical analysis and plant breeding.

This app is free for online use, just the intellectual property is reserved in periodicals
by scientific citations of Be-Breeder papers.
